About AZTU

Azerbaijan Technical University (AzTU), established in 1950 and based in Baku, is a leading technical institution providing engineering, technology, and management education. It features over 10,000 students across 8 faculties, offering programs in Azerbaijani, Russian, English, and German, with a strong focus on research, industry partnerships, and international cooperation.

Academic Structure: The university includes 7-8 faculties focusing on areas such as Mechanical Engineering, Information and Communication Technologies, Transport and Logistics, and Economics.
